# Business Requirements Document

## 1. Project Vision
**RetailNova Decision Intelligence** is an enterprise-grade decision support platform designed to empower business leaders with accurate, timely, and actionable insights across the entire organization. The platform aims to transform fragmented operational data into meaningful business intelligence, enabling faster, smarter, and more informed decision-making.

By integrating data from key business functions—including sales, finance, marketing, inventory, supply chain, customer support, and marketplace operations—the platform provides a unified view of organizational performance. It combines automated analytics, business KPIs, predictive forecasting, and AI-generated recommendations to help stakeholders identify opportunities, detect risks early, and understand the root causes behind business outcomes.

The long-term vision is to evolve beyond traditional reporting by building an intelligent decision intelligence platform that not only explains *what* has happened, but also predicts *what* is likely to happen and recommends the best course of action. Ultimately, RetailNova Decision Intelligence will serve as the company's single source of truth, supporting sustainable growth, operational excellence, and data-driven strategic planning.

## 2. Company Overview
**RetailNova** is an omnichannel retail enterprise founded in 2004 and headquartered in Mumbai, India. The company operates through a network of over 50 retail stores, 20 warehouses, an e-commerce platform, and a marketplace ecosystem connecting more than 8,000 sellers with customers across the country.

With an annual revenue of approximately **₹1,200 Crore**, RetailNova serves over 2 million customers through multiple sales channels, offering a wide range of products across Electronics, Fashion, Home & Kitchen, Beauty, Sports, Books, Furniture, Toys, and Grocery. The company employs more than 2,000 people across retail operations, supply chain, customer support, finance, marketing, and corporate functions.

### Business Model & Data Scale
RetailNova follows an omnichannel business model that enables customers to shop seamlessly through physical stores, online platforms, and marketplace sellers. The company also offers a premium membership program, **NovaPremium**, which provides exclusive benefits such as free delivery, priority support, cashback offers, faster returns, and early access to promotional campaigns.

As the business continues to expand, RetailNova generates large volumes of data from sales transactions, customer interactions, inventory movements, marketing campaigns, financial operations, logistics, supplier networks, and marketplace activities. Converting this growing volume of data into meaningful business intelligence has become a strategic priority for the organization to maintain sustainable growth and remain competitive in the retail industry.

## 3. Business Problem
RetailNova has experienced continuous growth in revenue, customer base, product offerings, and business operations over the years. However, as the organization has expanded, its operational and analytical processes have become increasingly fragmented. Business data is generated across multiple departments—including Sales, Finance, Marketing, Inventory, Supply Chain, Customer Support, Warehousing, and Marketplace Operations—making it difficult to obtain a unified and accurate view of overall business performance.

### Key Pain Points
* **Siloed & Manual Processes:** Although the company collects large volumes of operational data every day, much of the reporting process remains manual, time-consuming, and department-specific. Executives often rely on multiple reports prepared by different teams, resulting in delayed decision-making, inconsistent business metrics, and limited visibility into the root causes of business issues.
* **The Profitability Paradox:** One of the most significant challenges facing RetailNova is that while revenue and order volume continue to increase, overall profitability has shown signs of decline. Leadership lacks a centralized platform capable of identifying the factors contributing to reduced profit margins, such as rising logistics costs, inefficient inventory management, increasing product returns, ineffective marketing campaigns, supplier performance, pricing strategies, and changing customer behavior.
* **Reactive Risk Management:** The organization lacks proactive monitoring of business risks. Potential issues such as declining customer retention, stock shortages, excess inventory, supply chain disruptions, margin erosion, and operational inefficiencies are often identified only after they begin affecting business performance. This reactive approach limits the organization's ability to respond quickly to changing market conditions.

> **The Need:** RetailNova requires a centralized Decision Intelligence Platform that consolidates enterprise data into a single source of truth, automates business reporting, continuously monitors key performance indicators, identifies risks at an early stage, generates predictive insights, and provides AI-assisted recommendations.

## 9. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The RetailNova Decision Intelligence Platform will monitor a comprehensive set of KPIs across all major functional departments to establish functional clarity and unified metric calculations.

### 9.1 Executive & Leadership KPIs
* **Total Revenue:** Gross sales across all channels minus direct customer returns/refunds.
* **Gross Profit & Net Profit:** Raw profitability margins before and after operational, tax, and inventory expense cuts.
* **Profit Margin (%) & Revenue Growth Rate (%):** Critical indicators tracking core margin adjustments and scaling velocities.
* **Business Health Score & AI Risk Score:** Composite metrics monitoring enterprise stability and flagging early market/operational anomalies.
* **Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT) & Forecast Accuracy (%):** Measures capturing user market retention and forecasting model precision.

## 11. Risks & Assumptions
Successful implementation of the RetailNova Decision Intelligence Platform requires identifying and managing potential business and technical risks.

### Business Risks
* Inaccurate or inconsistent business data.
* Resistance to adoption by business users.
* Changing business priorities.
* Poor decision-making due to incorrect interpretations of analytics.

### Technical Risks
* Poor data quality from operational systems.
* Data integration challenges across multiple sources.
* Scalability limitations as business data grows.
* Performance issues with large analytical workloads.

### Operational Risks
* Delays in data availability.
* Failure of automated ETL processes.
* Incomplete KPI calculations.
* Dashboard downtime affecting executive reporting.

### Security Risks
* Unauthorized access to confidential business information.
* Data privacy and compliance issues.
* Data loss due to system failures.
* Cybersecurity threats targeting enterprise systems.

### Risk Mitigation
* Implement automated data validation.
* Maintain a centralized data warehouse.
* Enforce role-based access control (RBAC).
* Perform regular backups and disaster recovery testing.
* Continuously monitor system performance and data quality.
