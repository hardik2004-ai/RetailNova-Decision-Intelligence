# RetailNova Enterprise Data Dictionary

## Purpose

The **Data Dictionary** serves as the central reference for RetailNova's enterprise data platform. It standardizes business terminology, technical metadata, KPI definitions, naming conventions, and data governance rules to ensure consistency across SQL analytics, Power BI dashboards, Machine Learning models, and the AI Decision Engine.

---

# Objectives

* Establish a common business vocabulary across all departments.
* Standardize data definitions and technical metadata.
* Improve data quality and consistency.
* Support enterprise reporting and analytics.
* Enable reliable Machine Learning feature engineering.
* Provide trusted inputs for AI-assisted decision making.

# Business Glossary

## Revenue

**Definition**

Total value generated from completed customer sales before deducting operational expenses.

**Business Owner**

Finance

---

## Net Revenue

**Definition**

Revenue after deducting discounts, refunds, and cancelled transactions.

**Business Owner**

Finance

---

## Gross Profit

**Definition**

Net Revenue minus Cost of Goods Sold (COGS).

**Formula**

```
Gross Profit = Net Revenue − COGS
```

**Business Owner**

Finance

---

## Profit Margin

**Definition**

Percentage of revenue retained as gross profit.

**Formula**

```
Profit Margin = (Gross Profit / Net Revenue) × 100
```

---

## Active Customer

**Definition**

A customer who has completed at least one purchase during the previous 365 days.

---

## Churned Customer

**Definition**

A customer with no completed purchase during the previous 365 days.

**Version 1 Logic**

Rule-based.

Future versions may use predictive churn models.

---

## High-Value Customer

**Definition**

Customers belonging to the top 10% based on lifetime revenue.

---

## Average Order Value (AOV)

**Definition**

Average amount spent per completed order.

**Formula**

```
AOV = Net Revenue / Number of Orders
```

---

## Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

**Definition**

Estimated total revenue generated by a customer throughout their relationship with RetailNova.

**Version 1**

Historical CLV.

**Future Enhancement**

Predictive CLV.

---

## Return Rate

**Definition**

Percentage of sold items that are returned.

**Formula**

```
Return Rate = Returned Quantity / Sold Quantity
```

---

## Inventory Turnover

**Definition**

Measures how efficiently inventory is sold.

**Formula**

```
Inventory Turnover = COGS / Average Inventory Value
```

---

## Stock-Out

**Definition**

A situation where customer demand exists but available inventory reaches zero.

---

## Dead Stock

**Definition**

Inventory that has not been sold for more than 180 days.

---

## Marketplace Seller

**Definition**

A third-party business selling products through the RetailNova platform.

---

## NovaPremium Member

**Definition**

A customer enrolled in RetailNova's premium membership program.

# Data Quality Principles

RetailNova follows the following data quality principles:

* Accuracy
* Completeness
* Consistency
* Validity
* Timeliness
* Uniqueness
* Referential Integrity

All analytical datasets should satisfy these principles before being loaded into the Enterprise Data Warehouse.
