# Business Glossary

## Revenue

**Definition**

Total value generated from completed customer sales before deducting operational expenses.

**Business Owner**

Finance

---

## Net Revenue

**Definition**

Revenue after deducting discounts, refunds, and cancelled transactions.

**Business Owner**

Finance

---

## Gross Profit

**Definition**

Net Revenue minus Cost of Goods Sold (COGS).

**Formula**

```
Gross Profit = Net Revenue − COGS
```

**Business Owner**

Finance

---

## Profit Margin

**Definition**

Percentage of revenue retained as gross profit.

**Formula**

```
Profit Margin = (Gross Profit / Net Revenue) × 100
```

---

## Active Customer

**Definition**

A customer who has completed at least one purchase during the previous 365 days.

---

## Churned Customer

**Definition**

A customer with no completed purchase during the previous 365 days.

**Version 1 Logic**

Rule-based.

Future versions may use predictive churn models.

---

## High-Value Customer

**Definition**

Customers belonging to the top 10% based on lifetime revenue.

---

## Average Order Value (AOV)

**Definition**

Average amount spent per completed order.

**Formula**

```
AOV = Net Revenue / Number of Orders
```

---

## Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

**Definition**

Estimated total revenue generated by a customer throughout their relationship with RetailNova.

**Version 1**

Historical CLV.

**Future Enhancement**

Predictive CLV.

---

## Return Rate

**Definition**

Percentage of sold items that are returned.

**Formula**

```
Return Rate = Returned Quantity / Sold Quantity
```

---

## Inventory Turnover

**Definition**

Measures how efficiently inventory is sold.

**Formula**

```
Inventory Turnover = COGS / Average Inventory Value
```

---

## Stock-Out

**Definition**

A situation where customer demand exists but available inventory reaches zero.

---

## Dead Stock

**Definition**

Inventory that has not been sold for more than 180 days.

---

## Sales Channel

**Definition**

The platform through which a customer places an order.

**Supported Values**

- STORE
- WEBSITE
- MOBILE_APP
- MARKETPLACE

## NovaPremium Member

**Definition**

A customer subscribed to RetailNova Premium.

**Billing Options**

- Monthly (₹149)
- Yearly (₹1499)

RetailNova currently offers only one membership plan.

# KPI Dictionary

| KPI | Formula | Business Owner | Refresh Frequency |
|------|---------|----------------|-------------------|
| Revenue | SUM(total_amount) | Finance | Hourly |
| Net Revenue | Revenue − Refund Amount | Finance | Hourly |
| Gross Profit | Net Revenue − COGS | Finance | Hourly |
| Profit Margin | Gross Profit / Net Revenue | Finance | Hourly |
| Average Order Value | Revenue / Number of Orders | Sales | Hourly |
| Inventory Value | SUM(quantity_on_hand × unit_cost) | Supply Chain | Daily |
| Inventory Turnover | COGS / Average Inventory Value | Supply Chain | Daily |
| Return Rate | Returned Items / Sold Items | Operations | Daily |
| Customer Lifetime Value | Lifetime Revenue | Analytics | Weekly |
| Active Customers | Customers with purchases in last 365 days | Analytics | Daily |
| Churn Rate | Churned Customers / Active Customers | Analytics | Weekly |
| Procurement Spend | SUM(total_amount) | Procurement | Daily |
| Membership Revenue | SUM(membership_fee) | Marketing | Daily |
