# 🛍️ RetailNova Decision Intelligence

> An enterprise-grade Decision Intelligence Platform for an omnichannel retail company that transforms business data into actionable insights, predictive analytics, and AI-powered executive recommendations.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

RetailNova Decision Intelligence is a business-first analytics platform built for a fictional omnichannel retail enterprise. Unlike traditional dashboard projects, this platform is designed to help executive leadership understand business performance, identify operational risks, forecast future trends, and make data-driven strategic decisions.

The project simulates how a real enterprise integrates data from multiple departments—including Sales, Finance, Marketing, Inventory, Supply Chain, Customer Support, and Marketplace Operations—into a single decision-support platform.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Despite increasing revenue and customer growth, RetailNova is experiencing declining profitability. Business information is fragmented across multiple departments, resulting in delayed reporting, inconsistent metrics, and limited visibility into the root causes of business performance.

The Decision Intelligence Platform addresses these challenges by centralizing enterprise data, automating analytics, and providing AI-assisted recommendations for business stakeholders.
